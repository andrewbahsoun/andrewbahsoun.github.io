Reducing bias in Healthcare AI Models

(August 2024 - Current)

I research project I recently joined to help reduce bias in healthcare ML models. These biases can affect marginalized groups by concluding that Black patients are healthier than equally sick White patients, falsly predicting heart disease probabilities, assigning black women a higher chance of having postpartum depression, and more. We plan on using an In-Processing method on the ML models to decrease the bias. This page will be updated as our research continues.
